Rata has been creating and showing various types of art since 1991. He has shown in Boca Raton Florida, Washington D.C., Columbia South Carolina, and Seattle Washington. His primary medium is oil on canvas, and he has produced over 100 paintings.

His work in oil is a study of color and technique, and an attempt to capture pure emotion using those colors and techniques.
